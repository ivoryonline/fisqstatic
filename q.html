WHAT IS q ?

q is simply an idea that involves:

  • Social Equality
  • Right to Privacy
  • Liberty to be Yourself
  • Anti Fascism
  • Anti Homophobia & Transphobia
  • The Belief that All Life is Equal

q is not:

  • Composed of Economical Beliefs
  • Anarchy
  • Idealistic (q will give up freedoms if it means a more positive outcome)

q is decentralised, anyone that believes in everything listed is part of q, and any ideaology that agrees with everything listed is part of q.

q does not promote or endorse extremism.

You may notice these ideas are vague, that is because q is a wide-idea and no singular movement, there can be many interpretations though the general beliefs should remain the same.

The internet is a useful tool; you can replace characters such as "g", "c", "e" with "q" in online aliases to show support.
